Distinguished Performance Award for E3SM Team

19 Jul 2019

The Los Alamos team was awarded a 2018 Large Team Distinguished Performance Award in ALDSC & ALDGS for the Energy Exascale Earth System Model (E3SM) model, which was released in early 2018. I lead next generation development for coastal waves in this project.
